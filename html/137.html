The American Verse Project is a collaborative project between the University of Michigan Humanities Text Initiative (HTI) and the University of Michigan Press. The project assembled an electronic archive of volumes of American poetry prior to 1920. The full text of each volume of poetry was converted into digital form and coded in Standard Generalized Mark-up Language (SGML) using the TEI Guidelines, with various forms of access provided through the WWW.

Rights and Permissions

The University of Michigan Library provides access to these materials for educational and research purposes. These materials are in the public domain.
