Glossae.net Has Joined WhenYouNeedGod.com

By Mark Smith
Welcome to my blog! My name is Mark and I’m thrilled to have this opportunity to share my journey with you. From a young age, I’ve been actively involved in my church, serving in various leadership positions.
Published July 13, 2023
Share

Over the years, Glossae was an important Christian online resource that provided well researched glossary (or glosses) and commentary on the Bible and Latin Bible from the middle age period. Sadly, the original website went defunct but now the Glossae website is part of WhenYouNeedGod.com – the go-to resource for new Christians learning about God as they start their journey.

The content on glossae.net explains indepth and provides a different yet complementary perspective to the commentary you will find on this site.

In French

Au fil des années, Glossae a été une ressource chrétienne en ligne importante qui fournissait des gloses et des commentaires bien documentés sur la Bible et la Bible latine de la période médiévale. Malheureusement, le site Web original est devenu obsolète, mais maintenant le site Glossae fait partie de WhenYouNeedGod.com – la ressource incontournable pour les nouveaux chrétiens qui souhaitent en apprendre davantage sur Dieu au début de leur parcours.

Le contenu sur glossae.net explique en profondeur et offre une perspective différente mais complémentaire aux commentaires que vous trouverez sur ce site.
