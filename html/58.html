Electronic Editions of the Gospel According to John in Greek, Latin, Syriac and Coptic

Most recent edition

  • Transcriptions and Apparatus of the Gospel According to John

    Transcriptions of Greek, Latin and Coptic manuscripts of John prepared in connection with the Editio Critica Maior. These are the most up-to-date files, which include links to the raw XML for each witness.

  • Initial collation for the Editio Critica Maior of John

    A collation of the witnesses selected for the Editio Critica Maior. Please note that a revision is in progress. In addition to the link above, the first draft negative apparatus has also been archived on the ePapers repository.

Other editions on this website

  • The International Greek New Testament Project edition of the Greek majuscule manuscripts of John

    Electronic edition corresponding to the printed edition published in 2007.

  • Vetus Latina Iohannes

    An electronic edition of the manuscripts with Old Latin versions of John, presented as a verse-by-verse synopsis.

  • The United Bible Societies Byzantine Text of John

    Electronic edition corresponding to the printed edition published in 2007.

  • Coptic John

    Electronic transcriptions of manuscripts of the Sahidic and the minor Coptic versions of John

  • Family 1 in John

    Electronic edition by Alison Welsby comprising transcriptions and positive and negative apparatus of members of Family 1 in the Gospel according to John
