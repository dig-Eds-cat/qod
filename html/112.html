Oregon Petrarch Open Book

This website is an archive of the Oregon Petrarch Open Book, a National Endowment for the Humanities-funded project. NEH funding helped make the first phase of this project possible with additional support from many individuals in the years following.

Technical changes since its inception have made a perfect replica of the original site impossible to achieve, but we have attempted to preserve as much of the intellectual content as possible. Materials digitized as part of the grant are made available in their entirety in Oregon Digital; the collection is linked below.

View the Web Archive

The original Oregon Petrarch Open Book was captured using the University of Oregon’s ArchiveIt tool on August 23, 2023.

View the Digital Collection

Ten manuscripts were digitized as part of the Oregon Petrarch Open Book and made openly available for study through the Oregon Digital repository.

Questions or comments shared here will be sent to the Primary Investigator for the project, Professor Massimo Lollini.
