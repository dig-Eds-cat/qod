Welcome

Welcome to the Journal of Emily Shore: Revised and Expanded, edited by Barbara Timm Gates. Shortly after our print edition was issued in 1991, two manuscript volumes of Emily Shore’s journals surfaced and were acquired by the University of Delaware Library. This digital edition, newly edited by Prof. Gates, integrates the 1991 edition with transcriptions of the manuscript material.
